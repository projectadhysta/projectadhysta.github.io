                    </h2>

                        <p class="mbr-text mb-5 pt-3 mbr-light mbr-fonts-style display-5">

                        </p>
                        <p class="mbr-text mb-5 pt-3 mbr-light mbr-fonts-style display-5">

                        </p>

                            2. Skala kecil

                        </p>
